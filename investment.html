Common Questions

Before you reach out.

How much does a wedding photographer cost in New Hampshire?

At MT Frame Studio, wedding collections begin at $3,400 for intimate weddings and elopements and rise to $8,800 for full-weekend coverage with albums. Pricing depends on coverage hours, travel, and whether an associate photographer is included. We serve Alton, the Lakes Region, the White Mountains, and all of New England.

Where is MT Frame Studio located?

MT Frame Studio is based in Alton, New Hampshire, on the southern shore of Lake Winnipesaukee. We travel throughout the Lakes Region, the White Mountains, coastal New England, and beyond for weddings and destination sessions.

Do you photograph destination weddings outside of New England?

Yes. While we're based in Alton, NH, we regularly travel for weddings and engagements — from Newport, Rhode Island to red-rock ceremonies in Utah. Travel fees are quoted custom and added to any of our collections.

How long does it take to receive my wedding gallery?

Wedding galleries are delivered within 6–8 weeks of your wedding date. You'll receive sneak-peek images within 72 hours. Portrait and maternity galleries are typically delivered within 2–3 weeks.

How many weddings do you book each year?

We deliberately take a limited number of weddings each year — typically fewer than 20 — so every couple receives our full attention, both on the day and through the editing process.

Do I get the raw images or just edited ones?

Every collection includes a fully curated, edited gallery delivered through a private online heirloom gallery with a print release. We do not release unedited RAW files — every image in your gallery is hand-edited to match our signature tone.
