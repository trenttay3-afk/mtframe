The Photographers

Hi, we're Megan & Trent.

We're a married photography duo based in Alton, New Hampshire, and we shoot every wedding, portrait, and maternity session together. Two sets of eyes means we catch both the vow and the reaction to the vow. The sweeping frame and the quiet one, at the same time.

We believe in the quiet spectacle of ordinary days. Our couples tell us they feel more like themselves in front of our cameras than they do in the mirror. That's the point.
